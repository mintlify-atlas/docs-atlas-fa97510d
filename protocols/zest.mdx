**Lending** — Zest Protocol specializes in Bitcoin-backed lending and borrowing, offering transparent on-chain rates without intermediaries.

## Key Features

### Transparent On-Chain Rates
All interest rates are determined algorithmically and visible on-chain, ensuring complete transparency for lenders and borrowers.

### Bitcoin-Native
Zest is built specifically for Bitcoin DeFi, leveraging sBTC (Stacks-wrapped Bitcoin) to bring lending functionality to the Bitcoin ecosystem.

### Low Risk Profile
With conservative loan-to-value ratios and robust liquidation mechanisms, Zest maintains a low-risk profile suitable for Bitcoin holders seeking steady yields.

### Institutional-Grade Security
Zest employs battle-tested smart contracts and regular security audits to protect user funds.
