## Overview

**Bitflow** is a decentralized exchange aggregator on Stacks that ensures you always get the best swap rates for sBTC and STX trades. By routing orders across multiple DEXs, Bitflow optimizes execution and provides competitive yields for liquidity providers.

## Protocol Type

**DEX** (Decentralized Exchange) — Bitflow aggregates liquidity across Stacks DEXs to provide optimal swap rates and yield opportunities.

## Key Features

### DEX Aggregation
Bitflow automatically routes your trades across multiple decentralized exchanges to find the best execution price.

### Optimized Swap Rates
No need to manually compare DEXs — Bitflow does the work for you, ensuring maximum value on every trade.

### Liquidity Provision
Provide liquidity to sBTC/STX pools and earn 12.4% APY through trading fees and protocol incentives.

### Low Slippage
By aggregating liquidity, Bitflow minimizes slippage even on larger trades.

### Bitcoin-First Design
Purpose-built for sBTC trading on Stacks, optimizing for Bitcoin DeFi use cases.

## Risk Assessment

### Risk Level: Medium

Bitflow is rated **Medium Risk** due to:

- **Impermanent loss** — Liquidity providers exposed to IL on volatile pairs
- **Smart contract risk** — Aggregation adds complexity
- **Market volatility** — sBTC/STX pair subject to price fluctuations
- **Strong TVL** — $31.7M demonstrates market confidence
- **DEX aggregation** — Diversifies risk across multiple protocols
- **Active monitoring** — Regular security assessments

<Tip>
For lower risk, use Bitflow primarily for swaps rather than liquidity provision, or provide liquidity in smaller amounts.
</Tip>
