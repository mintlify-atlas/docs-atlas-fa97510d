# Welcome to Staxiq

Staxiq is a **professional-grade Bitcoin DeFi dashboard and AI Copilot** built natively on the Stacks L2 blockchain. It provides real-time insights, yield optimization, and portfolio management for Bitcoin-integrated DeFi protocols.

<Note>
  **100% Non-Custodial** - Staxiq only reads your on-chain data. Your keys stay with you, your funds stay in your wallet. We never ask for private keys.
</Note>

## What is Staxiq?

Staxiq connects to your Stacks wallet and shows you exactly where your STX and sBTC are working across every Bitcoin DeFi protocol — stacking, lending, trading, earning — all on one screen. With AI-powered strategy recommendations, you can maximize your yield securely while maintaining full control of your assets.

## Technology Stack

Staxiq is built with modern web technologies and blockchain infrastructure:

- **Frontend**: Vite + React + Tailwind CSS
- **Blockchain Interface**: @stacks/connect, @stacks/transactions
- **Data Visualization**: Recharts
- **API Layer**: Express (Blockchain data proxy)
- **AI Engine**: Gemini AI for strategy generation

## Security Principles

<Warning>
  Staxiq is **completely non-custodial**. All transactions are signed locally via your wallet provider (Leather or Xverse). We never have access to your private keys or funds.
</Warning>

Every action you take:

1. **Initiated by you** - No automatic transactions
2. **Signed in your wallet** - Full control over approvals
3. **Transparent on-chain** - Verifiable on Stacks Explorer
4. **Reversible by you** - Withdraw anytime from protocols
