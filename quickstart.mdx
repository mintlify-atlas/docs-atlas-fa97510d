## Connect Your Wallet

To access your real portfolio and interact with DeFi protocols:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install a Stacks Wallet">
    You'll need a Stacks-compatible wallet. We recommend:
    
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Leather Wallet" icon="chrome" href="https://leather.io">
        Browser extension wallet with clean UI
      </Card>
      <Card title="Xverse" icon="mobile" href="https://xverse.app">
        Mobile and browser wallet with Bitcoin support
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
    
    <Accordion title="Need help setting up a wallet?">
      Visit our [Wallet Setup Guide](/wallet-setup) for detailed instructions on installing and configuring your Stacks wallet.
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Connect Wallet">
    On the Staxiq homepage or dashboard, click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top navigation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Wallet">
    Choose your installed wallet from the popup menu:
    - Leather
    - Xverse
    - Hiro Wallet
    - Or other Stacks-compatible wallets
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Approve Connection">
    Your wallet will open asking you to approve the connection. Click **"Connect"** to authorize Staxiq to:
    - View your wallet address
    - View your STX and token balances
    - View your transaction history
    
    <Warning>
      Staxiq cannot move funds or sign transactions without your explicit approval each time.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Your Dashboard">
    Once connected, you'll see your real portfolio data:
    - Live STX and sBTC balances
    - Total portfolio value in USD
    - Recent transaction history
    - Detected protocol positions
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Explore Protocols

Discover DeFi protocols available on Stacks:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Protocol Explorer">
    Navigate to the **"Protocols"** page from the main navigation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter by Type">

    - **All** - View all protocols
    - **Lending** - Lend and borrow assets
    - **DEX** - Swap tokens
    - **Yield** - Liquidity mining
    - **Stacking** - Stack STX to earn BTC
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sort by APY">

  <Step title="View Protocol Details">
    Each protocol card shows:
    - Current APY
    - Total Value Locked (TVL)
    - Supported assets (STX, sBTC, etc.)
    - Risk level
    - Brief description
    - Direct link to protocol website
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my wallet safe when I connect?">
    Yes! Staxiq uses standard Stacks Connect authentication. We can only:
    - **Read** your public wallet address
    - **Read** your balances and transaction history
    
    We **cannot**:
    - Move your funds
    - Sign transactions without your approval
    - Access your private keys
    
    Every transaction requires explicit approval in your wallet.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need STX or sBTC to use Staxiq?">
    No! You can explore Staxiq with demo mode or connect an empty wallet. However, to:
    - Interact with DeFi protocols
    - Execute AI strategy recommendations
    - Earn yield
    
    You'll need to hold STX, sBTC, or other Stacks tokens.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does the AI generate strategies?">
    Staxiq uses Google's Gemini 1.5 Flash to analyze:
    - Your current portfolio (STX, sBTC balances)
    - Your selected risk profile
    - Available DeFi protocols with APY and TVL data
    - Your transaction history (to personalize advice)
    
    The AI generates personalized recommendations in 2-5 seconds, tailored to your experience level.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What does 'anchored on Bitcoin' mean?">
    Every AI strategy you generate is:
    1. Hashed using SHA-256
    2. Recorded on the Stacks blockchain
    3. Automatically anchored to Bitcoin
    
    This creates a verifiable, tamper-proof record of your strategy that's secured by Bitcoin's proof-of-work.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Which networks does Staxiq support?">
    Staxiq supports:
    - **Stacks Mainnet** (production, real funds)
    - **Stacks Testnet** (testing, no real value)
    
    Your connected wallet determines which network you're using. We automatically detect and display the appropriate address.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use Staxiq on mobile?">
    Yes! Staxiq is fully responsive and works on:
    - Desktop browsers
    - Mobile browsers
    - Tablet browsers
    
    For the best mobile experience, use a wallet with mobile support like Xverse.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
